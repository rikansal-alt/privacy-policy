Data Deletion Instructions

If you would like to delete your data from Instagram Ads Agent, please follow these instructions.

How to Delete Your Data

  1. Log in to your account at http://localhost:3000
  2. Go to Account Settings in the application
  3. Click "Delete My Account" or "Request Data Deletion"
  4. Confirm deletion - Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted

Alternatively, you can contact us directly using the contact information below.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will permanently delete:

  • Your user account information
  • Your Facebook/Meta access tokens
  • Your ad account associations
  • Campaign data stored in our system
  • Analytics data stored in our system
  • Any other personal information associated with your account

Contact Us

If you need assistance with data deletion or have questions, please contact us:

Email: [Your contact email here]

Note: Please include your Facebook User ID in your request to help us locate your account. You can find your Facebook User ID in your account settings.

Important Notes

  • Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes as required by law
  • Deleting your account will not affect your Facebook account or ad campaigns running on Facebook
  • We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receipt
  • After deletion, you will no longer be able to access your account or any stored data

Verification

To verify your identity and protect your data, we may request:

  • Your Facebook User ID
  • Your registered email address
  • Additional verification information as needed
